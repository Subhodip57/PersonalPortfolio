Behind the Code

I'm a 19-year-old Computer Science undergraduate currently in my 4th semester at Memari College. While my coursework is heavily rooted in core operating systems, Java, and DBMS, my curiosity extends far beyond the standard curriculum into the mechanics of security and systems.

You'll often find me experimenting within my Kali Linux environment, exploring network vulnerabilities like Evil Twin attacks, or bridging the gap between software and the physical world through hands-on Arduino and ESP8266 hardware projects.

I believe in a strong balance between mind and body. When I step away from the keyboard, I'm highly focused on hitting the gym, cutting video edits in Premiere Pro and After Effects, or unwinding with Ghost of Tsushima.
